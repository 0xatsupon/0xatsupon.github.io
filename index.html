Quantitative Finance

Optimal LP Range Strategies for Concentrated Liquidity

Extends Cartea et al.'s theoretical framework to derive optimal range widths for USDC/JPYC stablecoin and SUI/NS volatile pairs. Validated through Monte Carlo simulation with empirical volatility data.

Quantitative Finance

Optimal Rebalancing Triggers via No-Trade Zone Theory

Proposes a novel framework for determining when to rebalance liquidity positions, incorporating gas costs and market microstructure. Includes sensitivity analysis for varying fee tiers.

Argues that genuine artificial general intelligence requires endogenous, hedonically valenced drives — not just optimization targets. Explores the moral implications of machines that truly want.
